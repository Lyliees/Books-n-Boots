Our afternoon English Club at booksnboots offers a unique learning experience for children. Combining English language development with physical fitness, our program aims to promote overall well-being.

We provide diverse activities and materials, including textbooks, novels, films, and more, to explore various themes in the English world.

We collaborate with ZŠ Jitřní – škola s rozšířenou výukou TV, Praha 4, since 2018, our goal is to foster student development both in and out of the classroom.

By engaging with native speakers, children develop conversation skills while staying active through sports and movement. At booksnboots, we believe in the importance of a healthy lifestyle for a child's growth and overall development.

Join our club for a holistic and enriching English learning experience.
