Our suburban camp, Sports and English Adventure, offers a dynamic and immersive experience for children. Combining sports activities with English language learning, this camp promises a fun and educational summer adventure. Here's an overview of the daily program and what children should bring:

Daily Program:

Each day at Sports and English Adventure begins at 8:00 AM and concludes at 4:00 PM. The schedule is packed with exciting activities designed to engage both the body and the mind.

Morning Session (8:00 AM - 12:00 PM):

The day kicks off with energizing sports activities such as soccer, basketball, and swimming. Children will have the opportunity to enhance their physical skills, teamwork, and sportsmanship.

Language and Cultural Enrichment (12:00 PM - 1:00 PM):

After a well-deserved break, the campers will delve into immersive English language sessions. Through interactive games, role-plays, and cultural discussions, they will improve their language skills and broaden their global awareness.

Afternoon Adventure (1:00 PM - 4:00 PM):

The afternoon is dedicated to exciting outdoor adventures and team-building activities. From thrilling treasure hunts to nature exploration, children will develop resilience, problem-solving abilities, and create lasting memories.

What to Bring:

To make the most of their experience at Sports and English Adventure, children should come prepared with the following items:

  • Comfortable sports attire and athletic shoes
  • Swimwear and towel for swimming sessions
  • Sunscreen and a hat for sun protection
  • Packed lunch and snacks (nut-free)
  • Refillable water bottle
  • English notebook and pen

At Sports and English Adventure, we strive to provide a well-rounded experience that fosters physical, intellectual, and social development. Through the combination of sports and English language immersion, children will gain valuable skills, make new friends, and create cherished memories that will last a lifetime. Join us for an unforgettable summer adventure!
